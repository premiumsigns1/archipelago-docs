For Fishing Charter Operators

Commercial fishing in Maltese waters means navigating Transport Malta's Commercial Vessel Certificate \(CVC\) requirements, EU fishing regulations, passenger safety rules, and MFSA insurance obligations — all simultaneously. Most operators build documents from scratch or use generic templates that don't reference the specific laws Transport Malta inspectors actually look for.

The Malta Fishing Charter Compliance Kit contains 8 documents that each reference the specific regulation they satisfy: S.L. 425.01, S.L. 425.07, EU Directive 98/41/EC, and EU Regulation EC 1967/2006. When you walk into a Port State Control inspection, you can point to the exact document.
