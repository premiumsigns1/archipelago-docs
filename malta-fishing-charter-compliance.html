Malta Commercial Vessel Certificate \(CVC\) Requirements

All commercial fishing charter vessels operating in Maltese territorial waters must hold a valid Commercial Vessel Certificate issued by Transport Malta. The certificate requires compliance across several regulatory areas:

  • Safety equipment — Life jackets, distress signals, fire extinguishers, first aid kit per S.L. 425.07 requirements
  • Passenger documentation — Full passenger manifest including name, nationality, and ID for all passengers
  • Catch reporting — Species, weight, and fishing area logged per EU Regulation EC 1967/2006
  • Insurance — Third party liability insurance meeting MFSA minimum coverage requirements
  • Vessel documentation — Transport Malta registration, fishing licence from Department of Fisheries and Aquaculture

Key Malta Fishing Regulations

The following legislation applies to commercial fishing charter operations in Malta:

  • S.L. 425.01 — Fishery Regulations: fishing methods, licence requirements, minimum sizes
  • S.L. 425.07 — Fishing Vessels Regulations: safety equipment, vessel requirements, manning
  • EU Regulation EC 1967/2006 — Minimum fish sizes, catch limits, protected species
  • EU Directive 98/41/EC — Passenger manifest requirements for vessel operations
  • MFSA guidelines — Minimum insurance requirements for commercial vessel operators
